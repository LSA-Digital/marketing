# Research: Intermediary Channels for Home Health Coding & RCM Buyer Introductions

**Topic:** Relationship-rich channels, associations, consultancies, and M&A intermediaries in medical coding, revenue cycle, coding education/training, and home health care
**Problem Statement:** Identify BDA-like firms and adjacent buyers — especially in home health coding and training — that could produce warm buyer introductions and move faster than MEDICODAX
**Date:** 2026-03-11
**Status:** Complete

---

## Executive Summary

The home health coding and RCM advisory ecosystem is tightly networked around a small number of dominant consulting firms (SimiTree, McBee/Netsmart), a few boutique M&A advisors with proven home health deal flow (LECO, VERTESS, Greenberg Advisors), and a set of association/education platforms (AHCC/DecisionHealth/AHIMA, OASIS Answers, HPS) whose advisory boards and conference circuits are the primary relationship-building venues. The fastest path to warm buyer introductions is through the **consulting firms that already serve as M&A advisors to PE-backed post-acute platforms** (SimiTree, McBee) and the **boutique banks that have closed home health RCM deals in the last 12 months** (LECO, VERTESS). The conference circuit — particularly DecisionHealth's Home Care Leadership Summit and the OASIS Answers COS-C/CHS-C Conference — is the highest-density venue for meeting decision-makers.

## Strategic Observations

1. **SimiTree is the most active acquirer** in the exact space — they have PE backing, a stated buy-and-build mandate, and have already done 4 add-ons in coding/RCM/education. They are both a potential buyer AND a warm introduction channel to Sheridan Capital Partners' deal pipeline.

2. **LECO closed a home health RCM billing deal in May 2025** ("Project CORE") — they have the most directly relevant recent deal flow and are the right size for a lower-middle-market coding/training business.

3. **AHIMA is now a strategic acquirer** in coding education (acquired HCPro/DecisionHealth/AHCC in Oct 2023). Their corporate development team is a direct buyer conversation.

4. **The AHCC advisory board is the relationship map** — it includes SimiTree and McBee leaders. Getting onto the AHCC advisory board or presenting at a DecisionHealth event puts you in the same room as the buyers.

5. **OASIS Answers and HPS are founder-owned independents** — they are both potential acquisition targets themselves AND warm introduction nodes to the buyer community.

6. **Netsmart (McBee's parent) is a strategic acquirer** in post-acute IT and professional services — they acquired McBee in 2019 and are a potential buyer of adjacent coding/training businesses.
