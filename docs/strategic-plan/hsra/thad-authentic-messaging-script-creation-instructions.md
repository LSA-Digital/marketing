# Dr. Thad Perry - authentic messaging scripts (1-minute videos)

## Content

I need to create several short video scripts for Dr. Thad Perry to community his authenticity and experience in the area of assessing and improving community health.  Dr. Perry's core messaging is already baked into pophealthmap.com , so read that website and consider how he might communicate that in a < 1 minute video.

We will create several videos that target different audiences.  While we may have marketing and sales objectives for outreach to various audiences, it is important that Dr. Perry's messaging does NOT sound "salesy", it should be more about community health assessment and "having a measurable impact".

## 3-layer approach

In general, the approach is in 3-layers:

- Layer 1:  Broad population health assessment (state, county, census tract - using public data, such as 2022 census and ACS data)
- Layer 2:  Targeted (neighborhood/household) health assessments, using private data set with monthly updated data
- Layer 3:  Deployment of resources to help communities take action and improve their health outcomes (e.g., Clinical Care Navigators setting up mobile offices)

The "outcome" messaging will be slightly different, depending on the audience we are targeting the message towards.

### Audience 1 State/local health departments 

We are trying to figure out how to help state governments prioritize resources (and where) to spend the ~$200M that each state just received for rural health improvement.  See @HSRA-marketing-and-sales-briefing.md document to understand this target audiences' marketing strategy, and the kind of messaging that needs to come from Dr. Perry.

This audience is most closely aligned with https://pophealthmap.com messaging.

### Audience 2 State/local departments of commerce 

State/local departments of commerce are interested in spurring economic activity.  This is aligned with LSARS (https://lsars.com) so we can make permitting go faster, with a community incentive-based approach.  

This audience is indirectly aligned with https://pophealthmap.com messaging, becuase community health is important part of economic health.

### Audience 3 Permit requestors - developers, utility companies, construction, etc.

Permit requestors want to get their permits approved faster, and are interested in reducing community friction.  This is also a target audience of https://lsars.com. 

This audience is indirectly aligned with https://pophealthmap.com messaging, becuase if permit requestors want their permit approved faster, they are going to have to "give somethign back" to the community, and community health is important part of the "give back".
