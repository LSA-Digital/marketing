# LSA Digital Seattle Revenue Sprint (Feb 11–Mar 29, 2026)

## Glossary

- **AI**: Artificial Intelligence
- **CPS**: Community Permitability Score (LSARS metric for confidence of hitting a target permit approval date)
- **CRE**: Commercial Real Estate
- **DoD**: U.S. Department of Defense
- **HSRA**: Health & Social Risk Assessment
- **LSARS**: Life Science Analysis and Reporting Solution
- **PI**: Permit Intelligence
- **RAG**: Retrieval-Augmented Generation
- **SLU**: South Lake Union
- **USAF**: U.S. Air Force

---

## Strategic Objective

1. **Generate near-term custom app contracts** (small → mid-size builds).
2. **Attract investor interest** in LSA Digital and/or LSARS.
3. Position LSA as**“the best of the small contractors”**: enterprise-grade, regulatory-aligned, compliance-ready AI systems delivered fast.

---

## Categories

### Category C (Highest Priority): Buyers with Budgets

CRE developers, infrastructure operators, utilities, healthcare systems, regulated service providers, government departments.

### Category A: Funded Startups / Founders

Seed / Series A companies needing MVPs, AI integration, or investor-ready builds.

### Category B: Gov / Defense / Enterprise

Agencies, primes, subcontractors, enterprise innovation teams.

## Core Messaging Positioning at Events

### 10-Second Hook

“We build enterprise-grade, compliance-ready AI applications—fast. Not demos. Production systems leaders can trust.”

### 30-Second Buyer Pitch

“We’ve built regulatory-aligned systems for the U.S. Air Force. Now we build AI-first applications that are grounded, auditable, and defensible. Our LSARS platform shows how AI can accelerate permitting and reduce community opposition using data-backed confidence scoring.”

### Close Fast Strategy

Offer:

- 10-day AI Proof Sprint
- 2–4 week Compliance AI Assistant
- 2–3 week Workflow Automation Pack

Always propose a **fixed scope + fixed timeline** next step.

## Execution Rule

Each event should produce:

- Minimum 3 qualified conversations
- 2 booked follow-ups
- 1 scoped opportunity within 7 days

Revenue momentum compounds if follow-up happens within 24 hours.
